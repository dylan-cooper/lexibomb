    Scoring

    Letter Points

    The letter points used by Lexibomb are based on the letter’s frequency in standard English writing; commonly used letters such as vowels are worth one point, while less common letters score higher.

    A - M
    Letter A B C D E F G H I J K L M
    Points 1 3 3 2 1 4 2 4 1 8 5 1 3
    N - Z
    Letter N O P Q R S T U V W X Y Z
    Points 1 1 3 10 1 1 1 1 4 4 8 4 10
    Blank _
    Points 0
    ### Tile Set The tile set used by Lexibomb consists of 100 tiles with the following distribution:

    Tile _
    Count 2
